## II. Exponential Function
$$
\begin{aligned}
e^x& = (2^{\log_2 e})^x\\
&= 2^{x\log_2e}\\
&= 2^{u + v}\\
&= 2^v \cdot 2^u\\
\therefore &= (1+v) \ll u
\end{aligned}
$$
